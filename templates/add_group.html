About Product Groups

Product groups help you organize related inventory items like product variations (different sizes, colors, etc.) under a single category.

Examples:
  • Group Key: "t-shirt-basic"
    Items: T-Shirt Basic (S, M, L, XL)
  • Group Key: "headphones-pro"
    Items: Headphones Pro (Black, White)
Tip: Groups are automatically created when syncing from platforms that have product variations.
