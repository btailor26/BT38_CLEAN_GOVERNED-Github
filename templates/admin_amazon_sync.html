About This Tool

This utility syncs Amazon MFN (FBM) quantities TO warehouse stock for items where:

  • Warehouse quantity is 0 or NULL
  • Amazon has actual stock > 0
  • Item is MFN (Merchant-Fulfilled) - FBA items are skipped

Note: After this sync, normal "Warehouse is Boss" rule applies - warehouse pushes to Amazon.
