📊 How These Numbers Are Calculated

  • Stock Value: Quantity × Unit Cost (from warehouse)
  • Revenue: Sum of selling prices for recent sales (last 100 transactions)
  • Cost of Goods Sold: Sum of unit costs for items sold
  • Net Profit: Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold
  • Profit Margin: (Selling Price - Unit Cost) ÷ Selling Price × 100
  • Low Stock Alert: Items where quantity ≤ reorder point
