Need Help Getting Credentials?
  1. Go to developer.ebay.com
  2. Sign in with your developer account
  3. Click "Your Account" → "Application Keys"
  4. Click "Create a keyset" if you don't have one
  5. Copy the App ID, Cert ID, and Dev ID
  6. Click "User Tokens" to get your access token
  7. Paste everything in the form above
