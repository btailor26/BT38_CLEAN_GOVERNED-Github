Quick Guide
Creating Listings
  1. Select a warehouse stock item
  2. Choose one or more marketplaces
  3. Enter listing details (title, price, etc.)
  4. Configure sync settings
  5. Click "Create Listing(s)"
Sync Settings

Sync Quantity: Keeps marketplace stock in sync with your warehouse automatically.

Quantity Buffer: Reduces the visible quantity on marketplaces to maintain safety stock.

Maximum Quantity: Prevents showing too much stock on marketplaces (useful for high-volume items).

Note: Listings are created in "pending" status. Use the "Push" function to publish them to actual marketplaces.
